Membership Level Benefits

NP Membership (No Fee):

Access to networking events with other non-profit organizations.

Listing on the Chamber's website as a non-profit member.

Opportunity to participate in community service projects sponsored by the Chamber.

Access to educational resources and workshops specifically tailored for non-profits.

Bronze Membership:

Access to networking events with other businesses and organizations.

Listing on the Chamber's website as a Bronze member.

Discounted rates for Chamber-sponsored events and workshops.

Opportunity to showcase business through Chamber's social media platforms.

Silver Membership:

All benefits of Bronze membership.

Priority placement in the Chamber's online business directory.

Enhanced visibility through featured articles in the Chamber's newsletter.

Opportunity to host or sponsor Chamber events.

Gold Membership:

All benefits of Silver membership.

Premier placement in the Chamber's online business directory with logo display.

Exclusive invitations to VIP events and receptions.

Dedicated account manager for personalized assistance with Chamber services.
